# Overview
## Problem Statement
Digital Special Collections and Access (DSCA) requires a report of all archival objects that have google drive digital objects.
## Goals

# Background
Google Drive links were originally used on certain archival objects due to a lack of proper place to store specific projects. They need to be updated.
